Life lessons from smokers

Generosity

You rarely see people split bills for cigarettes. This doesn't happen for anything else. You don't see people giving away free beer. But people lighting cigarettes for others , a very common scene .

Carefree-ness

Smokers know very well the affects of smoking and that it could kill them brutally. Yet smokers tend to slowly increase the amount they smoke and tend to buy cheaper cigarettes while counting down their days to live. They don't care about police fining them .They stop and smoke wherever they feel like irrespective of location. Be it in the middle of a highway or even in-front of an hospital, smokers are everywhere. They are very carefree.

Compassionate

The easiest way to find a cigarette shop in Kerala is look down outside the shop. You'll even find information on the brands they sell by the trails left by fellow smokers, saving you from the trouble of finding a place by yourself. Strangers light your cigarette for you.

Perseverance

Most chain-smokers you know are on the verge of quitting. They buy nicotine patches and smoking cigarettes. It's basically the equivalent of buying a mask and cutting holes in it.

Extremely good at excuses

Smokers become extremely good at excuses . They claim it helps them calm down and increases their creativity. Only to find themselves sitting in front of a screen writing an 'original' article that is awfully similar to the ones you read in Buzzfeed for instance this one .
